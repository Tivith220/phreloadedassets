# 🤖 PH ENERGON HORIZON (v2.0.0)

> **"Autobots, Ready to Roll... The Clock is Ticking."**

A minimalist, high-tech Transformers-themed battery monitor that lives on the edge. Designed to be a sleek "Energon Horizon" at the top of your screen.

## ⚡ Features (v2.0.0)

-   **Energon Horizon**: Move from a chunky widget to a minimalist 5px bar at the top of your primary display.
-   **Transformation Mode**: Hover your mouse at the top to see the bar "Deploy" (expand to 40px) and reveal detailed status and percentage.
-   **Autobot Sync**: The bar pulses with **Matrix Blue** when charging or healthy.
-   **Decepticon Alert**: The bar turns **Decepticon Purple/Pink** with a glitchy warning when battery is critical.
-   **Metallic HUD**: Modern, tech-heavy fonts and metallic textures.
-   **Mechanical Sounds**: Iconic transformation and alert sounds for power events.

## 🕷️ Installation

1.  Check the `dist` folder for the latest build.
2.  Download `PH Energon Horizon Setup 2.0.0.exe`.
3.  Run the installer. The "Horizon" will appear at the top of your screen.
